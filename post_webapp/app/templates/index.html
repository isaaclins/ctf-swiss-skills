About Us

POST is your premier partner for interstellar logistics, delivering cargo across planets, moons, and orbital stations with unparalleled efficiency—or at least, that’s what our AI overlords tell us to say. Founded in 3015 by a caffeine-addicted spacer who thought "shipping should be as fast as my coffee brews," we’ve been zipping packages across the galaxy ever since.

Our Mission

To connect the galaxy through reliable shipping, ensuring colonies thrive with timely supplies—and to boldly deliver where no drone has delivered before! Whether it’s freeze-dried Martian tacos or a spare warp coil, we’ve got your back (and your cargo hold).

Services

  • Cargo transport between Earth, Mars, and beyond—yes, even that sketchy asteroid belt bar.
  • Real-time tracking for all shipments—because who doesn’t love stalking their package like it’s a reality holo-show?
  • Specialized handling for rare minerals, perishables, and that one guy’s pet lunar rock collection.
